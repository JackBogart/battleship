Planning

Man your stations, this is war! To prepare for battle, place your ships on the planning board below. Drag and drop ships from your shipyard onto the grid below. Your choices aren't permanent, you can reposition as needed!

To rotate a ship, click on the ship after placing it on the board. Keep in mind you can't rotate a ship if it results in an invalid placement (i.e. out of bounds or overlapping another ship).
